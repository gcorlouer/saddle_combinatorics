\section{Goal}

Collect the action formulas needed for the proof of Conjecture A and prove the basic equivariance facts.

\section{Objects}

The key groups are:
\[
G_d=\prod_{\ell=0}^L \GL(d_\ell,\R),
\qquad
G_{\mathrm{hid}}=\prod_{\ell=1}^{L-1}\GL(d_\ell,\R),
\qquad
G_d^{W_0}=\{g\in G_d : g_0W_0g_L^{-1}=W_0\}.
\]

\section{Required lemmas}

\begin{lemma}
The action of $G_d$ on $\Omega$ satisfies
\[
\mu(g\cdot W)=g_L\mu(W)g_0^{-1}.
\]
\end{lemma}

\begin{lemma}
The hidden gauge group preserves the end-to-end map:
\[
\mu(g\cdot W)=\mu(W)
\qquad
\text{for } g\in G_{\mathrm{hid}}.
\]
\end{lemma}

\begin{lemma}
The cyclic locus $\Gamma_{d,S}$ is stable under $G_d^{W_0}$.
\end{lemma}

\begin{lemma}
The rank-$r$ cyclic locus $\Gamma_{d,S}^r$ is stable under $G_d^{W_0}$.
\end{lemma}

\section{Deliverables}

This task is complete once the file contains full proofs of the four lemmas above and an explicit statement explaining why $G_{\mathrm{hid}}$ alone cannot prove Conjecture A.
