\section{Goal}

Prove that the intersection of a $G_d^{W_0}$-orbit in $\Gamma_{d,S}^r$ with the critical locus is exactly one orbit of the correct stabilizer subgroup.

\section{Target statement}

\begin{lemma}
If $W,W'\in \mathcal C_{d,S}^r$ lie in the same $G_d^{W_0}$-orbit inside $\Gamma_{d,S}^r$, then the group element relating them preserves the distinguished product $P_SW^\ast$ and therefore belongs to
\[
H_S:=\Stab_{G_d^{W_0}}(P_SW^\ast).
\]
\end{lemma}
