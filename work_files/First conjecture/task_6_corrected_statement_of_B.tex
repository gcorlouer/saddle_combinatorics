\section{Final form after Task 0}

Task 0 shows that for $r>0$ the critical locus is not stable under $G_d^{W_0}$, so the correct statement is obtained by introducing the stabilizer subgroup
\[
H_S:=\Stab_{G_d^{W_0}}(P_SW^\ast)
\]
and then formulating the orbit correspondence as follows.

\begin{theorem}[Corrected form of Conjecture B]
Assume $r>0$. Then:
\begin{enumerate}
\item every $G_d^{W_0}$-orbit in $\Gamma_{d,S}^r$ meets the critical locus $\mathcal C_{d,S}^r$;
\item for every $W\in \mathcal C_{d,S}^r$,
\[
(G_d^{W_0}\cdot W)\cap \mathcal C_{d,S}^r
=
H_S\cdot W.
\]
\end{enumerate}
Equivalently,
\[
\Gamma_{d,S}^r/G_d^{W_0}
\cong
\mathcal C_{d,S}^r/H_S.
\]
\end{theorem}

\begin{remark}
The only case in which the old quotient
\[
\mathcal C_{d,S}^r/G_d^{W_0}
\]
survives literally is the degenerate rank-zero case.
\end{remark}
