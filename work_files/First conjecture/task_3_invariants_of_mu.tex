\section{Goal}

Extract all constraints on the end-to-end map
\[
M=\mu(W)
\]
that are forced by the cyclic equations
\[
W_{<\ell}W_0W_{>\ell}=0.
\]

\section{Questions to answer}

This file should address the following:

\begin{itemize}
\item Which image and kernel constraints on $M$ are visible from the cyclic equations?
\item Which constraints depend only on $M$, and which still depend on the full tuple $W$?
\item Which of these constraints are invariant under the endpoint group $K_S$?
\end{itemize}

\section{Required outcome}

The goal is to isolate a list of necessary conditions on $M$ for membership in $\Gamma_{d,S}^r$ and to prove a separation lemma of the form:

\begin{lemma}
The cyclic equations impose a family of constraints, some depending only on $M=\mu(W)$ and some depending on the full factorization $W$.
\end{lemma}

\section{Deliverables}

This task is complete once the file contains:

\begin{itemize}
\item a list of candidate invariants of $M$;
\item a proof of which ones descend to the end-to-end map;
\item a clear statement of the remaining information that still lives at the factorized level.
\end{itemize}
